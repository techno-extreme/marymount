About Marymount Library

Every effort has been made over the past two decades to develop a variety of library resources and world-class study facilities that complement classroom teaching and learning as well as research. Students and their instructors alike are welcome to take every advantage of the collections of carefully selected resources in various formats within the Marymount Library and beyond. Library staff are on hand to assist and instruct readers to master the techniques needed to become self-sufficient in accessing the resources available in their particular disciplines. The library aid so much in reaching the organisational goals.

MM Library seeks to support teaching, learning and research of academic professional excellence in the provision of quality entrepreneurial and science oriented primary teacher education.
